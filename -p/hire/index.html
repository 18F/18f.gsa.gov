Hire 18F

Let’s work together to tackle your agency’s technology problems and transform how you serve the public.

Have a project in mind? Want to see if 18F can help your agency? Email Dan Kenny on our Agency Partnerships team at inquiries18F@gsa.gov.

We’ll set up a time to talk more, answer your questions, and ask about what you have in mind. Once we understand your office and the problems you're trying to solve, we'll figure out whether we can help you build or buy a solution.

Email us

How we work with partners

18F is a cost-recoverable office, which means we don't receive appropriated funds from Congress and must charge our partner agencies for the work we do. We use Interagency Agreements to set up the terms of our projects.

You can read more about how we work with agencies in our Partnership Playbook.
